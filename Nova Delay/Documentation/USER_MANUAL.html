Quick Start

  1. Start in NEUTRAL.
  2. Choose a target low frequency with LOW FREQ.
  3. Add weight with LOW BOOST or tighten lows with LOW ATTENUATION.
  4. Pick a top-end frequency and add clarity using HIGH BOOST.
  5. Use BANDWIDTH to make the boost broader or more focused.
  6. Smooth harshness using HIGH ATTENUATION.
  7. Level-match with OUTPUT GAIN.

Control Reference

LOW FREQChooses which low region gets shapedSelects the low-shelf turnover frequency: 20, 30, 60, or 100 Hz.

Preset Buttons

The preset buttons are recall buttons, not hidden DSP modes:

  • NEUTRAL — flat starting point
  • VOCAL — clarity and presence
  • BASS — classic low-end weight + control
  • AIR — smooth top-end shine
  • MASTER — broad mix polish
These presets move the actual EQ settings. They do not add secret processing behind the scenes.

Under the Hood

Nova Tone combines a low-shelf boost stage, low attenuation stage, high boost stage, and high attenuation stage. After the EQ network, it adds a light analog-style saturation pass so larger tone moves stay rich and smooth instead of brittle.
