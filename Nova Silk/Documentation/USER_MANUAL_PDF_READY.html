Nova Silk User Manual

Spectral Refinement Engine

Nova Silk smooths harsh spectral energy while preserving clarity, body, and musical detail.

Controls

ControlFunction
SMOOTHSets overall smoothing intensity.
FOCUSShifts where smoothing is concentrated in the spectrum.
AIR PRESERVEProtects high-frequency openness while reducing harshness.
BODYMaintains low-mid fullness during smoothing.
OUTPUTApplies final gain trim for level matching.

Magic And Presets

MAGIC changes behavior profile (more polished ON, more neutral OFF).

Presets: UNIVERSAL SMOOTH, VOCAL, DRUMS, GUITAR, MASTER.

Spectral Analyzer

The analyzer shows real-time incoming energy, detected problem zones, and reduction movement.

The analyzer is visual feedback only and does not directly alter audio by itself.
