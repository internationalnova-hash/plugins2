# Nova Master — Style Guide v1

## Identity
Nova Master is the **final stage** in the Nova lineup.

If:
- `Nova Tone` = shaping
- `Nova Heat` = color
- `Nova Level` = control

then:
- `Nova Master` = **finish**

## Emotional Tone
The plugin should communicate:
- precision
- clarity
- confidence
- refinement
- “mastering environment” energy

## Color Direction
### Base
Keep the Nova family champagne background so the plugin still belongs to the suite.

### Accent shift
Instead of warm gold as the main accent, shift toward:
- soft platinum
- brushed silver
- cool ivory / cool white highlights

### Gold usage
Reserve warm gold mostly for:
- `FINISH` active state
- very small moments of premium emphasis

This creates the right contrast:
> **silver precision + gold magic**

## Layout Feel
Nova Master should be:
- wider
- cleaner
- less busy
- more spacious than the other Nova plugins
- clearly horizontal and mastering-oriented

The feeling should be:
> luxury mastering hardware

## Knob Language
### Required differences from the other plugins
- slightly thinner ring
- slightly sharper indicator
- less glow
- cooler metal body
- more refined spacing between knobs

These knobs should feel like precision mastering controls, not color-box controls.

## Meter Language
The meter is a key identity area.

It should feel:
- cool
- accurate
- clean
- slightly blue-white
- more “measurement” than “energy”

Avoid overly warm or saturated meter colors for the main state.

## Finish Button Identity
This is the one place where warmth should appear strongly.

When `FINISH` is ON:
- use a warm gold glow
- add inner highlight
- make it feel rare, intentional, premium

The message is:
> everything is precise… except the magic.

## Typography
### Title
- elegant serif treatment for `NOVA MASTER`
- slightly more premium and stately than the other plugins

### Labels
- uppercase sans-serif
- clean spacing
- cooler white / neutral labeling

## Final Polish Priorities
For the flagship pass, push the UI slightly further than the rest of the suite:
- cool the silver treatment by another **5–10%**
- give each knob a more sculpted mastering-hardware pedestal
- make the output rail feel like a calibrated final-stage module
- let `FINISH` carry the strongest glow and premium contrast
- keep the overall impression restrained, expensive, and finished

## Final Read
When a user opens Nova Master, they should feel:
> **This is the final stage. Don’t mess this up.**
