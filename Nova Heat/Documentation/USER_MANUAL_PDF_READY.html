Nova Heat User Manual

Harmonic Saturation & Energy Processor

Nova Heat adds warmth, density, bite, and excitement without falling into harsh distortion.

Controls

ControlDSP Function
DRIVERaises the level hitting the saturation stage.
TONEApplies tilt-style tonal balance and focus shaping.
HEATIncreases harmonic density, curve intensity, and transient softening.
MIXControls dry/wet blend.
OUTPUTApplies final output trim.

Modes

SOFT, MEDIUM, and HARD are real DSP character modes that change drive multiplier, saturation curve behavior, transient response, and stereo spread.

MAGIC

When enabled, MAGIC adds finishing polish: more upper-mid focus, smoother top control, extra dynamic sensitivity, and a touch more width.

MAGIC changes the backend sound but does not move the main knobs by itself.
