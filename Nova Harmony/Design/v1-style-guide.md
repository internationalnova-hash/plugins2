# Nova Heat — Style Guide v1

## Design Intent
Nova Heat should visually sit beside Nova Tone, but with a slightly hotter and more energetic identity.

**Key feeling words:**
- warm
- analog
- premium
- energetic
- polished
- mix-ready

## Palette
| Role | Hex | Notes |
|------|-----|-------|
| Main faceplate | `#EED9BB` | slightly hotter champagne base with more energy |
| Rich faceplate shadow | `#E4CCA8` | deeper warmth for a more active feel |
| Inner panel | `#D7BA90` | stronger panel depth and contrast |
| Dark text | `#4A4036` | main labels |
| Soft text | `#6F6253` | supporting copy |
| Primary gold | `#C68D1E` | hotter family gold accent |
| Rich amber | `#C27612` | more energetic Heat accent |
| Pale gold | `#E6BF67` | brighter highlights and arc tones |
| Signature copper | `#A85C3B` | used for the `HEAT` wordmark and `by International Nova` branding |
| Cream white | `#FFFAF4` | reflections and top light |

## Typography
### Title
- Font stack: `Iowan Old Style`, `Palatino Linotype`, `Georgia`, serif
- Weight: bold
- Style: classic boutique mastering hardware

### Subtitle / Section Labels
- Font stack: `Avenir Next`, `Helvetica Neue`, `Arial`, sans-serif
- Style: uppercase with generous letter spacing
- Tone: precise and premium

### Value Readouts
- Font stack: `Avenir Next`, `Helvetica Neue`, `Arial`, sans-serif
- Weight: semibold/bold
- Readable at a glance

## Knob Style
- Champagne/gold metal body
- Fine radial detailing
- Soft top-left highlight
- Warm amber indicator line
- Ring accent should intensify on the `HEAT` knob

### Hero Knob Rule
The `HEAT` knob is the signature control and should be:
- larger than the other center controls
- more strongly highlighted with amber/gold contrast
- the first thing the eye lands on after the title

## Buttons
### Curve buttons (`SOFT`, `MEDIUM`, `HARD`)
- Small pill-style buttons
- Neutral cream when inactive
- warm amber/gold when active

### Preset buttons (`VOCAL`, `DRUMS`, `BASS`, `MASTER`)
- Larger bottom-row pills
- Same visual family as Nova Tone
- Active state should glow slightly warmer than Nova Tone

## Metering
- Use vertical LED bars
- gold at normal levels
- richer copper-amber toward hotter states
- should feel subtle and premium, not like a harsh EDM UI

## Spacing & Layout Rules
- Outer padding: `24–34px`
- Section gaps: `14–18px`
- Rounded corners throughout
- Keep control clusters centered and balanced
- Avoid overcrowding the output panel

## Design Difference vs Nova Tone
**Nova Tone:** shaping / elegant / refined  
**Nova Heat:** character / richer / hotter / more energetic

So compared with Nova Tone:
- slightly warmer champagne hue
- slightly stronger panel contrast
- richer gold and amber accents with a touch more drive and energy
- a muted signature copper for the `HEAT` wordmark and byline
- more visual emphasis on saturation and output activity
