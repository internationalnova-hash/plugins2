# Nova Tone — Style Guide v1

## Brand Feel
`Nova Tone` should feel like boutique analog mastering hardware: warm, elegant, tactile, and expensive. It should clearly belong to the `Space By Nova` family while leaning more into classic EQ hardware than ambient luxury.

---

## Core Palette

### Faceplate & Panel
- **Main faceplate:** `#F2E8DC`
- **Backup faceplate:** `#EFE4D6`
- **Richer option:** `#EDE0CF`
- **Inner panel:** `#E4D6C3`

### Text & Engraving
- **Primary text:** `#4A4036`
- **Secondary text:** `#6F6253`
- **Muted etched labels:** `rgba(74, 64, 54, 0.68)`

### Accent Metals
- **Primary gold:** `#C89A43`
- **Soft gold:** `#D8B56D`
- **Highlight gold:** `#E5C986`

---

## Layering & Depth

### Top Highlight
- thin white gradient at `10–15%` opacity
- should feel like soft reflected light on metal

### Bottom Shadow
- warm soft shadow around `10–20%` opacity
- adds physicality and panel weight

### Divider Styling
- subtle etched lines
- avoid strong web-style borders
- prefer warm transparency over stark contrast

---

## Knob Language
- rounded analog-style knobs with warm ivory centers
- gold arcs and marker dots
- tactile but restrained, not overly futuristic
- slightly different personality from `Space By Nova`: more classic EQ hardware, less dreamy luxury polish

### Knob Behavior Feel
- broad, smooth, confidence-inspiring
- should visually suggest tone shaping, not surgical control

---

## Buttons

### Preset Buttons
- large pill buttons
- warm neutral inactive state
- gold active state with soft depth and gentle glow
- text should remain readable and premium, not flashy

### Frequency Selectors
- segmented switches / capsule chips
- subtly raised from the panel
- selected state should be warm gold with elegant contrast

---

## Typography Rules
- section titles should be uppercase with added tracking
- never use pure black for labels
- strong hierarchy:
  - brand title
  - section titles
  - knob labels
  - readouts / helper text

---

## Design Goal
When a user opens `Nova Tone`, it should feel like:

> **"This is a real analog-inspired tone box built for records."**

Not:

> **"This is a generic digital EQ plugin."**
