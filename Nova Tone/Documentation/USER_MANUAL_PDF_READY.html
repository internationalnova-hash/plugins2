Nova Tone User Manual

Musical Tone Shaping Equalizer

Nova Tone is a broad, musical EQ designed for weight, clarity, air, and polish rather than surgical correction.

Quick Start

  1. Start in NEUTRAL.
  2. Choose the target low region with LOW FREQ.
  3. Shape weight using LOW BOOST and LOW ATTENUATION.
  4. Use the high section for presence, clarity, and air.
  5. Match loudness with OUTPUT GAIN.

Controls

ControlDSP Function
LOW FREQSelects the low-shelf turnover frequency.
LOW BOOSTAdds broad low-shelf boost for weight and warmth.
LOW ATTENUATIONApplies broad low-shelf cut for tighter low end.
HIGH BOOST FREQSelects the center frequency for top-end boost.
HIGH BOOSTAdds presence or air using a peak filter.
BANDWIDTHAdjusts Q of the high boost stage.
HIGH ATTENUATION FREQSelects where top-end softening begins.
HIGH ATTENUATIONApplies high-shelf attenuation to smooth harshness.
OUTPUT GAINApplies final level trim.

Preset Buttons

NEUTRAL, VOCAL, BASS, AIR, and MASTER are recall buttons that set the actual EQ values to musical starting points.

They do not add hidden extra processing beyond the visible settings.
