Space By Nova User Manual

Studio Glow for Modern Vocals

Space By Nova is a vocal-friendly ambience designer focused on width, bloom, and depth with minimal controls.

Visible Controls

ControlDSP Function
SPACERaises room feel, bloom delay, motion depth, and tail development.
MIXControls the final dry/wet balance.

Mode Buttons

STUDIO, ARENA, DREAM, and VINTAGE are real DSP voicing modes that change room feel, width, damping, early reflections, and motion.

The mode buttons also recall hidden internal ambience settings such as Air, Depth, and Width balances.
