Spectral Analyzer

The analyzer is a live visual feedback display that shows overall energy movement, tail behavior, and width bloom.

It helps with faster decision-making while shaping space, but does not directly change DSP by itself.

Mode Buttons

ModeFeelBackend Change
STUDIOTight and controlledSmaller room feel, shorter pre-delay, stronger ducking, less width and motion.
ARENABigger and widerLarger room, more pre-delay, more side width, more halo and movement.
DREAMSoft and lushMost diffuse space, longer bloom, fewer early reflections, more motion and tail lift.
VINTAGEDarker and narrowerMore damping, less width, darker top end, less halo and motion.
These are real DSP voicing modes, not just display labels.

Quick Start

Start in STUDIO, raise SPACE, then shape character with AIR, DEPTH, and WIDTH. Finish with MIX for placement.
